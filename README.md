# CBIT Connect

## Overview

CBIT Connect is a student collaboration platform designed to help students at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) find teammates, collaborators, and project partners for hackathons, startup ideas, technical projects, competitions, and events.

Many students miss opportunities because they cannot find the right team members. CBIT Connect aims to bridge this gap by creating a dedicated platform where students can connect based on their skills, interests, and goals.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often face challenges such as:

* Finding teammates for hackathons and competitions.
* Connecting with students from different branches and years.
* Discovering people with complementary skills.
* Building teams for startup ideas and projects.
* Missing opportunities due to lack of collaboration.

CBIT Connect provides a centralized solution to help students build meaningful connections and successful teams.

---

## Features

### Team Discovery

* Find students interested in similar projects and events.
* Search for collaborators based on skills and interests.

### Collaboration Opportunities

* Connect with students across different departments and academic years.
* Form teams for hackathons, projects, and startup initiatives.

### Student Profiles

* Showcase skills, interests, and project experience.
* Help students identify suitable teammates.

### Opportunity Sharing

* Share information about hackathons, competitions, events, and project opportunities.
